# Kamdem final QA checklist

Kamdem's remaining review is intentionally limited to concise visual and user-experience feedback after technical gates pass.

## Public sites

- [ ] Confirm the Emerson Empire homepage hierarchy, spacing, and mobile navigation feel correct.
- [ ] Confirm the Emerson Agency contact section is visible and the message button opens the expected request modal.
- [ ] Confirm the EPDG landing page navigation, class content, and mobile layout remain visually coherent.
- [ ] Flag any outdated copy, image, visual credit, or visibly broken destination; do not independently replace destinations or change technical configuration.

## EPDG platform

- [ ] Confirm logged-out private routes show the sign-in experience rather than dashboard content.
- [ ] Confirm unavailable/empty/error states are understandable and do not resemble live fixture data.
- [ ] Confirm forms communicate validation and backend-unavailable states clearly.
- [ ] Check desktop and mobile navigation labels for clarity.

## Handoff format

Provide one short list with: page, device size, screenshot, expected visual result, and observed issue. No backend, database, deployment, credential, or migration work is assigned to Kamdem during finals.
